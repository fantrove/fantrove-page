### New

- **Multi-language support (English / Thai)**
  The site now supports two languages with a language switcher. Users can toggle between English and Thai, and their preference is saved in localStorage. All UI text, navigation labels, and content adapt automatically.

- **Language proxy and sync system**
  A language proxy (lang-proxy.js) and sync system (lang-sync.js) ensure that language state is consistent across all pages and scripts. Language changes trigger a custom event that all components listen to.

### Improved

- **Navigation bar responsiveness**
  The top navigation bar now adapts better to small screens with improved layout scaling and touch-friendly tap targets.

- **Page transition smoothness**
  Page transitions have been refined to reduce visual jank and provide a smoother experience when navigating between sections.
