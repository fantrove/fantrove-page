### New

- **New emoji categories added**
  Additional emoji categories including activities, food and drink, travel, and objects. Users can now browse a much wider selection of emojis organized by theme.

### Improved

- **Search performance optimization**
  Emoji search now uses an optimized indexing approach that reduces lookup time significantly, especially for large queries. The search results appear faster and feel more responsive.

- **Category navigation redesign**
  The category sidebar has been redesigned with better visual hierarchy and clearer labels, making it easier to navigate between different emoji groups.
